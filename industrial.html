The Rescue Facility

In order to provide tourists with a safe and comfortable environment on the sea, the rescure facility is designed for drowning, and provide a floodlight that rotates 360 degrees in the night.

When drowning people press a button on the buoy, carbon dioxide will fill the facility instantly and the buoy will pop out to provide a safe environment. Meanwhile, it also provide oxygen to drowning victims who choke on the water with a small tank.

A'Design Award - 01 Convenience

Home

Because of it incorporates urban sewage, Convenience can be placed anywhere with a sewer line and manhole cover. The team have got a brilliant idea in reusing the manhole cover. The grooved-putting cover can make the product balance during service time. And the unique appearance make the pass water will be discharged into sewage well directly by the urinal so that it will not store urine. Convenience can be self-cleaning on rainy days. It is so light that can be moved easily by municipal workers.
